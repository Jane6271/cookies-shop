Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I place my order?

For standard orders, we recommend placing your order at least 24 hours in advance. For large orders (more than 5 dozen cookies) or custom orders, please provide at least 48 hours notice.

Do you offer gluten-free options?

Yes! We offer a selection of gluten-free cookies made with almond flour. Please note that while we take precautions to prevent cross-contamination, our kitchen is not a completely gluten-free environment.

What is your delivery area?

We offer free delivery within a 10-mile radius of our bakery. For deliveries outside this area, we partner with local delivery services. Nationwide shipping is also available for our cookie gift boxes.

Can I customize my cookies for special events?

Absolutely! We love creating custom cookies for weddings, birthdays, corporate events, and holidays. Please contact us at least one week in advance to discuss your custom cookie needs.

How should I store my cookies?

For best results, store your cookies in an airtight container at room temperature. They will stay fresh for up to 5 days. For longer storage, you can freeze them for up to 3 months. Simply thaw at room temperature before enjoying.
