About Cookie Heaven

Our story, our passion, our cookies

Our Sweet Story

Cookie Heaven began in 2010 as a small family bakery with a simple mission: to create the most delicious cookies using only the finest ingredients. What started as a home-based business quickly grew into a beloved local institution.

Our founder, Grandma Rose, believed that cookies should be more than just a treat â€“ they should be an experience that brings joy and creates memories. Her secret recipes, passed down through generations, continue to be the heart of our bakery today.

Over the years, we've expanded our offerings but never compromised on quality. Every cookie is still handcrafted with the same love and attention to detail that Grandma Rose insisted upon.
